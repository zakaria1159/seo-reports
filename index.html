CRITICAL

1. Hreflang Inconsistencies

Affected Pages: 12 pages (32%)
The Problem:

French pages are tagged as English and vice versa. This breaks your bilingual SEO and shows wrong language to users.

The Fix:
  • Audit every hreflang tag in your HTML
  • Ensure French pages have hreflang="fr" and English pages have hreflang="en"
  • Add x-default to point to your primary language
Impact:

CRITICAL - This is killing your international SEO

CRITICAL

2. Internal 404 Errors

Broken Links: 4 internal URLs
The Problem:

Dead links on your website. Bad user experience and wastes crawl budget.

The Fix:
  • Find pages linking to these 404s in Screaming Frog's inlinks tab
  • Either fix the links or create 301 redirects
PART 1: CRITICAL FIXES

üéØ Missing Keyword Strategy

CRITICAL

3. No Keyword Research = Duplicate Content

Duplicate H1s: 14 pages (33%)
Duplicate H2s: 14 pages (33%)
The Root Problem:

You don't have a keyword map. Each page needs to target DIFFERENT keywords, but without research, you're using the same headings across multiple pages.

What's Happening:
  • Multiple pages fighting for the same keywords
  • Google doesn't know which page to rank for what
  • Your pages cannibalize each other's rankings
The Fix - Keyword Research Process:
  • Step 1: Use Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner
  • Step 2: Find 1 primary keyword per page (300+ monthly searches)
  • Step 3: Find 2-3 secondary keywords per page
  • Step 4: Create unique H1 with primary keyword
  • Step 5: Create unique H2s with secondary keywords
Impact:

CRITICAL - Without unique keywords per page, you can't grow traffic

PART 3: GROWTH STRATEGY

üîß Technical SEO for Growth

GROWTH OPPORTUNITY #3

Build a Strong Technical Foundation

1. Fix Your Site Architecture:
  • Create clear category hierarchies (3 clicks max from homepage)
  • Build topic clusters with pillar pages + supporting content
  • Add breadcrumb navigation with schema markup
  • Create an HTML sitemap for users
2. Improve Internal Linking:
  • Link from high-authority pages to new content
  • Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
  • Add "Related Articles" sections
  • Link from old content to new content
3. Schema Markup:
  • Add Organization schema to homepage
  • Add LocalBusiness schema if you serve specific areas
  • Add FAQ schema to pages with questions
  • Add BreadcrumbList schema for navigation
4. Speed Optimization:
  • Aim for Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1
  • Enable browser caching
  • Minify CSS/JS files
  • Use a CDN (you already are for images!)
